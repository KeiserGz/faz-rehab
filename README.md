## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Customize the content:
   - Replace "PhysioTherapy Plus" with your clinic name
   - Update contact information with your details
   - Modify services to match your offerings
   - Add your own testimonials from real patients

## Customization Guide

### Change Clinic Name
- In `index.html`: Update the `.logo` text
- Search for "PhysioTherapy Plus" and replace with your name

### Update Contact Information
- Find the Contact section in `index.html`
- Replace address, phone, email, and hours with your details

### Modify Colors
- Open `styles.css`
- Edit the `:root` color variables:
  - `--primary-color`: Main green color
  - `--secondary-color`: Dark green
  - `--accent-color`: Red accent color

### Add/Remove Services
- In `index.html`, duplicate or remove `.service-card` divs in the services section
- Change the emoji, title, and description

### Update Services Statistics
- Find the `.stat` divs in the About section
- Update the numbers and labels (e.g., "500+ Patients Treated")

### Customize Testimonials
- Replace testimonial quotes and author names in the Testimonials section
- Add or remove testimonial cards as needed
